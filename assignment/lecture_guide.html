üéØ Expected Results & Discussion

What to Expect:

‚úì Good Signs:
  • 60-85% validation accuracy
  • AI follows general strategy
  • Avoids ghosts when close
  • Collects seeds efficiently
  • Uses energizers sometimes
‚ö†Ô∏è Common Issues:
  • Gets stuck in corners/loops
  • Runs into ghosts in novel situations
  • Ignores energizers (if rare in training)
  • Jittery movement (action oscillation)
  • Low accuracy on rare actions

Discussion Questions:

Why does the AI fail in certain situations? ‚Üí Distribution shift: The AI encounters states that weren't present in the training data. During training, it only learned from states YOU encountered. When it makes mistakes and reaches new states, it has no experience to draw from.
How could we improve performance? ‚Üí Multiple approaches:
  • Record more diverse demonstrations (different strategies, paths)
  • Data augmentation (mirror game states horizontally)
  • DAgger algorithm (Dataset Aggregation) - iteratively collect data from AI's mistakes
  • Ensemble methods (train multiple models)
Why not just use reinforcement learning? ‚Üí Trade-offs:
  • BC advantages: Simpler, faster to train, no reward engineering, leverages expert demonstrations
  • BC limitations: Limited by expert performance, suffers from distribution shift, can't exceed expert
  • RL advantages: Can exceed expert, explores new strategies, handles novel situations better
  • RL challenges: Requires reward design, much slower training, needs lots of exploration
Where is behavior cloning used in practice? ‚Üí Real-world applications:
  • Autonomous driving: Learning from human drivers (Waymo, Tesla)
  • Robot manipulation: Learning from teleoperation demonstrations
  • Game AI: Learning from player logs and esports replays
  • LLM alignment: Learning from human feedback demonstrations (RLHF)
  • Surgical robotics: Learning from expert surgeon demonstrations
üìö Extend Your Learning:

Try implementing DAgger (Dataset Aggregation):
1. Train initial model on expert demonstrations
2. Let AI play and record states it encounters
3. Have expert label those states with correct actions
4. Add to dataset and retrain
5. Repeat until performance plateaus
